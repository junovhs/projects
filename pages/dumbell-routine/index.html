Proper Form

  • Hold dumbbell vertically at chest level, elbows down
  • Feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly turned out
  • Keep chest up and core engaged throughout movement
  • Lower by pushing hips back and down, knees track over toes
  • Descend to comfortable depth (thighs parallel to floor if able)
  • Drive through heels to return to starting position

Focus on even weight distribution. Reduce depth if knee discomfort occurs. Stop at any point where you feel stable and comfortable. Prioritize controlled movement over depth.

Proper Form

  • Stand with feet hip-width apart, slight bend in knees

  • Start with arm extended, pull elbow back close to body
  • Squeeze shoulder blade, lift elbow toward ceiling
  • Lower with control, feel stretch in back and shoulders

  • Stand tall with feet shoulder-width apart
