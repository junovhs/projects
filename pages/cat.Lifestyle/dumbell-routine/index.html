Full Body Dumbbell Routine

One 20lb Dumbbell • 3x Per Week

Workout Overview

30–40Minutes
8Exercises
3xWeekly
20lbDumbbell

Focus: Build muscle, improve posture, and burn fat with a progressive full-body plan designed for minimal equipment.

1

Goblet Squat

Legs • Glutes • Core
3 × 10–15

Proper Form

  • Hold dumbbell vertically at chest level
  • Lower slowly for 3 seconds, drive up with control
  • Keep chest up and core braced
2

Romanian Deadlift

Hamstrings • Glutes • Lower Back
3 × 12
  • Hold dumbbell in both hands in front of thighs
  • Hinge at hips, slight bend in knees
  • Lower until you feel hamstring stretch, then return
3

One-Arm Floor Press

Chest • Triceps • Shoulders
3 × 10 each side
  • Lie on floor, dumbbell in one hand
  • Press weight up until arm is straight
  • Lower slowly, keeping elbow at ~45°
4

One-Arm Overhead Press

Shoulders • Triceps • Core
3 × 8–10 each side
  • Start with dumbbell at shoulder height
  • Press straight overhead, avoid leaning
  • Lower under control
5

One-Arm Row

Back • Lats • Biceps
3 × 10–12 each side
  • Brace free hand on knee or chair
  • Pull dumbbell toward ribcage
  • Lower slowly and fully extend arm
6

Hammer Curl

Biceps • Forearms
3 × 10–12 each side
  • Hold dumbbell with palms facing in
  • Curl slowly, squeeze at top
  • Lower with control
7

Russian Twist

Obliques • Core
3 × 20 twists
  • Sit on floor, lean back slightly
  • Rotate torso side to side holding dumbbell
  • Control the motion, avoid swinging
8

Loaded Carry

Core • Grip • Posture
3 × 30–40 sec each side
  • Hold dumbbell at your side
  • Walk slowly with upright posture
  • Switch hands and repeat

Routine Complete!

Perform this workout 3x per week with at least 1 rest day in between. Track reps, add reps or slow tempo to progress. Combine with your IF schedule for best results.
